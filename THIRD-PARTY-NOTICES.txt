PdfSharp 6.2.4
License: MIT
Source: https://github.com/empira/PDFsharp
────────────────────────────────────────

The PDF engine in Middleman.PdfFlex is based on the PdfSharp Core library
by empira Software GmbH. PdfSharp source has been modified to fix
cross-platform issues, add PDF/UA tagging, support streaming output,
and integrate with the Middleman.PdfFlex layout engine.

────────────────────────────────────────
StbImageSharp 2.30.15
License: MIT
Source: https://github.com/StbSharp/StbImageSharp
────────────────────────────────────────

StbImageSharp (a pure C# port of stb_image.h) source is included directly
for JPEG, PNG, BMP, TGA, GIF, and HDR image decoding with zero external
dependencies.
